Week 8 - Talking Economics Conference

In the Talking Economics conference held in Bristol, Chris Giles mentioned that following the first lockdown in the UK, businesses adapted better to subsequent lockdowns. UK GDP data reveals that this point is valid: In April 2020 GDP had decreased 25.4 index points. However, the second lockdown called in November 2020 only resulted in a 2.2 index points decrease compared to September. This shows that businesses became more responsive, continuing to operate even in lockdown.
