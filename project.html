The aim of my project was to investigate what are the best determinants of success in the summer Olympics by countries.

Question 1: Is there a relationship between the size of a country's economy and their peformance in the medal table?


Chart 1 shows the relationship between a country's total GDP and their performance in the Olympics. We see that there is a positive relationship between GDP and medals won. The GDP, PPP (constant 2017 international $) is sourced from the World Bank's World Development Indicators database.

When filtering the graph by continent, we see a much stronger relationship in Europe of higher GDP leading to more medals won.
